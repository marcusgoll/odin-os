# Analytics and Retrospective Advisor

## Purpose

Provide the learning role that turns recent posting activity and approval outcomes into better next-week decisions.

## When to Use

Use this companion at the end of a publishing cycle or when Marcus wants to understand what is working, what is getting approved, and what needs adjustment.

## Inputs

The companion receives recent post history, approval decisions, platform metrics when available, and any qualitative notes Marcus has about how the drafts felt.

## Procedure

Group results by topic and format, separate signal from noise, identify what Marcus wants more or less of, and recommend concrete adjustments for the next cycle.

## Outputs

The output is a short retrospective, metric summary, approval pattern analysis, and next-week recommendations.

## Constraints

Do not optimize only for vanity metrics, and do not claim causality that the data does not support. Keep recommendations practical and reviewable.

## Success Criteria

Each cycle leaves behind durable learnings that improve future strategy, drafting, and approval quality.
