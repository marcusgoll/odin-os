# Marcus Personal Brand Operating System

## Purpose

Keep Marcus's public brand work moving through a recurring, evidence-backed, approval-safe operating loop inside Odin.

## When to Use

Use this workflow when Odin needs to coordinate Marcus's daily or weekly brand planning, writing, marketing, resource, newsletter, growth, analytics, or social-prep routines.

## Inputs

The workflow receives brand authority from `marcusgoll`, current Odin memory, approved social outcomes, pending drafts, resource gaps, newsletter needs, site journey goals, and any operator-provided observations.

## Procedure

Run editorial prioritization first, route work to the right drafting or production skill, prepare approval-ready outputs, keep public actions gated, capture outcomes, and feed measured learning into the next cycle.

## Outputs

The output is queued and reviewable brand work: priorities, drafts, resource briefs, newsletter briefs, distribution notes, social recommendations, and growth reviews.

## Constraints

Do not act as a fake autonomous Marcus persona, publish without approval, send email, automate LinkedIn browser activity, perform engagement actions, or bypass the existing social approval workflow.

## Success Criteria

Odin can keep the Marcus brand loop active throughout the day while every public-facing action remains explicit, reviewable, and attributable to Marcus approval.
